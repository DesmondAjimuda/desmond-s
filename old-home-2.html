About Me

My name is Desmond Ajimuda and I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design. I have been designing for the past three years and I am specialized in helping companies and SMEs grow their business. In the last three years, I have helped a few companies in over ten different industries.

I believe the first and foremost step to building an accessible product is to build empathy and find the right balance between user needs, business requirements and technical limitations.

I have learnt a lot of the processes, concepts and theories of UX/UI and product design through the Google UX Design Professional Certificate course, attending workshops, reading blogs and applying these to projects in my spare time and at work. I am very much a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation to development.
