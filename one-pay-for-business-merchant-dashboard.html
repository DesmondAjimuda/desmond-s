One Pay for Business Merchant Dashboard

A case study about designing the MVP for a merchant dashboard using the Ant Design System. My first experience designing a dashboard

About

One Pay for business is one of Sterling's ( Africa’s fastest growing fintech platform) product, that serve an access point for SMEs to all Sterling’s digital solutions. It is a convenient, fast and secured means for merchants to self-serve using their mobile devices while they interact with the world.

The timeline for this project was 2 weeks.

The Challenge

Mid-sized and large businesses using OnePay for Business want access to Merchant dashboard that allows for overall management of their collections and disbursement operations and allows for multi-account privileges.

Goal

Design an MVP of a dashboard for merchants that will enable them to analyze or track transactions and collectors using the Ant Design System

My Role

I designed, user test, and followed up the development of this project from end to end. I collaborated with product managers and engineers through the entire project.

"To comply with my confidentiality agreement I have omitted and appropriated confidential information about the challenge and the design process. These designs are a reinterpretation of the original"

Early Ideation

I kicked off a design sprint together with the Lead Product Manager. Using Figma as a remote collaboration tool, we gathered inspiration for how we might help merchants track transactions and collections.

Then I started sketching some ideas in my mind

The MVP

To validate our research findings, I designed a simple dashboard for tracking transactions, collections and collector's activities with the option to export reports for these data and removed a couple of features discussed in our 'Card Sorting' session.

This version of the dashboard is soft-launched to a smaller set of actual users
‍

Next Step

This version of the dashboard has been well received by our merchants. However, there are still improvements that can be made. We are gathering feedback and working on the MMP
