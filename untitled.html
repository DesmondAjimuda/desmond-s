About

I'm a product designer and Webflow developer, building the future of payment in Africa at Clane in Nigeria.

I have been designing for the past three years, and I'm specialized in helping companies and SMEs grow their business.

I believe the first and foremost step to building an accessible product is to build empathy and find the right balance between user needs, business requirements and technical limitations.

I have learned a lot of the processes, concepts and theories of UX/UI and product design through the Google UX Design Professional Certificate course, attending workshops, reading blogs and applying these to projects in my spare time and at work.
