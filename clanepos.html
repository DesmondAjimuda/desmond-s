About

ClanePOS, a mobile app POS. A revolutionary innovation that allows merchants to collect sales, onboard and manage sub-merchants in one app, and generate extra revenue by using agency banking (as) added services.
‍
ClanePOS is changing the game by transforming POS from an obligatory piece of merchant equipment to a device enabling the merchant to enhance their services and revenue.

My Role

I led the design, user testing and development of this project from end to end. I collaborated with product designer, Joseph Burutu during the early ideation stage, product manager, Kasarachi Odor, Ibukun Adedeji and product designer Thanau Abass throughout the entire project

The problem

Small and large business merchants in Nigeria are looking for simple and affordable tools to start, grow and run their business, there is a lot of mechanical burden in the process of receiving money and monitoring business as a business owner, POS devices are expensive, quickly become outdated, and take a significant portion of the merchant’s revenue.

Giving merchants a simple and affordable tool to manage their business meant fundamental changes to the way merchants  receive payment.
Traditionally,  merchants will receive cash or charge the customers card using a POS device.

Moving away from the POS device which has become an obligatory piece of merchant equipment required a ground-up rethinking of the way merchants collect sales and manage their business.

Our solution

We created ClanePOS, a mobile app POS to help merchant, starts, grow and manage their business.
ClanePOS empower the merchant to;

  • Receive money using their mobile phone

  • Onboard and manage sub-merchant

  • Make extra money running agency banking as added services.

  • Increase revenue by reducing the cost of purchase and maintenance of the traditional POS device

Receive money

To help the merchant receive and confirm payment faster. I designed a 2-steps receive money flow that offers control and flexibility in the way it functions and looks.

We designed for merchant receive instant notification when customers scan their static QR code to pay or pay via bank transfer.

Withdraw money

Fast and easy way for merchant to withdraw money from store account in 2-steps.

A dashboard business management

I designed a dashboard that offers control and flexibility in the way it functions and looks.
The goal was to offer merchants full control over the way they manage their business.

Result

The MVP of this product has been well received by our merchants. However, there are still improvements that can be made. A merchant should be able to manage products, and send payment link.  We designed a foundation that allowed us to build on top of, adding in a home for global level features and functionality such as inventory. The patterns and designs created would later help inform future parts of the product as we constantly strive to improve our merchant experience.
