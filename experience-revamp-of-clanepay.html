Experience revamp of ClanePay

I was the lead designer for this project in Q1 - Q3 2022, ideating early concepts, running design sprints, regularly presenting to stakeholders, and delivering final assets for production. I worked with multiple cross-functional teams of 2 product managers, 4 engineers, and leads from Content, Growth, Brand and Marketing.

Project Overview

When a user download the ClanePay, they want to add money to their wallet and make payment for anything, anywhere using just their phone.

The current design often fails to provide users with what they need to know in order to make a decision quickly while trying to add money to their wallet, send money to a friend or pay a merchant in a store, resulting in fewer conversions and users losing trust in the platform.

The total redesign or rebranding to ClanePay serves to better match the user's mental model of making payment with a digital wallet.

The problem

We’ve heard from people over the years that the current ClanePay payment experience is stressful due to an uncategorized overload of features and a design that isn’t modern enough.

The goal

  • Empower users to quickly send money or add money to their wallet without stress, while optimizing usability.

  • Streamline the process for sending, loading & top-up, or paying bills.

  • Offer users discounts, referral programs, or games to earn credit for more engagement.

  • Build Trust through the brand and product.

  • Design fewer efforts to interact with the app.

New Design

A usable, modern, scalable and user-friendly product

We were able to come up with a redesign that addressed to a very large extent some of the issues we identified earlier.

Result

With the redesign, users more quickly add money to their wallet and send and receive money on ClanePay, especially with their Clanetag. In general, users felt confident to make a decision to add money to their wallet or send money to others after they familiarized themselves with the new Clanepay payment flow.
